The Ancient Aztec God

Xolotl's sacrafice

HEADLINE

It is believed in mexican mythology that the axolotl used to be a god. He's said to have turned himself into a type of salamander to avoid a self sacrafice. He and the other gods that created the fifth sun agreeed that there needed to be a self sacrafice in order to nourish the sun, that was stagnant in it's position in the sky.

The myth surrounding the unique Mexican axolotl involves Xolotl, an Aztec god determined to escape his impending sacrifice.
